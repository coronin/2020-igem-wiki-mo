Target group

Our project comes from the elderly calcium problem. China already has a population of 1.4 billion, and the economy develops rapidly. People are asking for a better life now. Among them, the bone problem is a problem we can not ignore.

China is an ancient civilization, and Chinese food culture is of great antiquity and well-known globally. However, China's dietary structure also has brought trouble for people's calcium problems. In the traditional diet of East Asians, including Chinese, vegetarians are the leading food, supplemented by meat, and rarely consume animal dairy products, leading to a low calcium intake. Some plants also contain many oxalates, phosphate, and other elements, which complex calcium ions and reduce calcium ions' absorption rate.

Statistics show that the average Chinese consumes less calcium than the recommended daily intake and many Chinese face serious bone problems. It is estimated that in 2006, there were nearly 70 million osteoporosis patients in China, and the number of bone loss has exceeded 200 million. Despite the lack of recent epidemiological data, estimates of osteoporosis and bone loss in China far exceed these figures. The problem of osteoporosis is more severe in the elderly, and there are deaths due to falls and fractures every year. Therefore, we focus our target population to the elderly.

Product form

Our project aims to design a genetically engineered probiotic that colonizes and secretes a short peptide in the gut. This short peptide binds to calcium ions to increase soluble calcium and increases the absorption rate of calcium in the intestine, thus achieving calcium supplementation.

Our products are basically positioned as probiotics health products. The relatively mature probiotics products in the market include probiotics lozenges, drops, powders, and capsules. And we chose to make our program available to the target population in the form of probiotic capsules. Compared with other forms of probiotics, the capsule has unique advantages, such as high bioactivity and mature technology. Besides, the commonly used capsule shell is plant fiber shell, which is less than the traditional gelatin capsule additive and more suitable for the elderly.

Commonly used probiotic lozenges can add more additives and adhesives, sweeteners, and molding needs heating, which will damage the probiotics' activity. Due to many additives, although easily accepted by children, older people won't care about whether the drug will taste good. A lot of additives may add the burden on the health of the elderly. Other forms such as drops or dry powder each have advantages, but there are not easy to storage, maybe a shortcomings of high cost, not a good choice for the elderly.

Considered safety aspects

General calcium supplements may increase the risk of cardiovascular diseases when taken in excess, but there is no need to worry about this due to the quorum sensing module in our product. The number of our engineered probiotics in the gut is strictly limited so they have little chance of causing intestinal flora disturbance. The Kill Switch module will be employed to effectively prevent leakage of bacteria into the natural environment. As for ethical risks, we imagine it will only occur when the product initiated clinical trials. We want to be very mindful of ethical risks about our product and ensure that we meet all requirements posed by the Chinese government as well as international ethical guidelines for conducting clinical research, including but not limited to informed consent, fair subject selection, confidentiality, and informing participants about new information. At that stage, we will also consult experts in this area and take our ethical practices a step further.

Our Kill Switch is a gene device to eliminate bacteria in a short period of time when engineered bacteria escape from the set condition 37 degree. Kill switches commonly are applied on account of three reasons, hindering gene vertical transfer, hindering gene horizontal transfer, and preventing the survival of bacteria outside certain environments. In order to ensure our engineered probiotic will not jeopardize the environment, we introduced a cold triggered toxin/antitoxin Kill Switch into our project to deprive of the survivability of engineered E. coli Nissle in the environment when excreted from the human intestine.

The ultimate goal of our project is to produce a commercial calcium supplementary probiotic to colonize in the intestines. An eco-friendly commercial probiotic should avoid the occurrence of modified gene vertical transfer and spread widely in the natural environment when excreted from the human intestine. And gene vertical transfer is directly associated with the survival of bacteria. Therefore, we needed to construct a Kill Switch to deprive of the survivability of engineered E. coli outside human bodies.

Simultaneously, we noticed that consumers may want to cease probiotics functioning because of accidental overdose, incidental gastrointestinal distress, or any other reasons. We could trigger our probiotic for rapid suicide by BBa_K3036005.

Also, concerning hindering gene horizontal transfer, we provided a potential solution other than introducing one more Kill Switch. Visit our Safety page to see our solution on the future expectation.
